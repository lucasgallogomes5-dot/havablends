Etymology and Meaning

The Origin of the
Name HAVA

The name “HAVA” carries an ancestral meaning linked to life, breath, and essence. Of Hebrew origin, derived from "Chavah" (חוه), it literally means "Life".

It is related to the name Eve, the source that generates life, creation, and a deep connection with the cycles of nature.

At HAVA Blends, this name reflects our purpose: transforming nature's "breath" — essential oils — into combinations that awaken memories and well-being in every existence.
